# 48 Hours Eating BBQ Across Texas

The plan was simple: eat as much BBQ as humanly possible across three Texas cities in 48 hours. No sightseeing. No museums. Just smoke.

## Day 1: Lockhart

Lockhart is the BBQ capital of Texas by law. Seriously — the state legislature said so. I hit Kreuz Market at 10am (cash only, no sauce, no apologies) and Black's right after. Both are mandatory.

## Day 1 Evening: Austin

Franklin's required an early morning commitment I wasn't ready for on this trip, so I went to La Barbecue instead. Zero regrets — the beef ribs are the size of your forearm.

## Day 2: Houston

Houston's BBQ scene is underrated. Gatlin's BBQ in the Heights does a smoked turkey that changed my life. Closed on Mondays — learned that the hard way.

## Final Tally

48 hours. 6 restaurants. Approximately 11,000 calories. Would absolutely do it again.
