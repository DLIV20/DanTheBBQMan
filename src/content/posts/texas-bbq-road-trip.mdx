# Sushi Masa in Metairie Is the Real Deal

You don't have to go far from New Orleans proper to find a seriously solid Japanese spot. Sushi Masa in Metairie has been holding it down and the crowd inside on any given night tells you everything you need to know.

## What They Do

Sushi Masa is a Japanese seafood and fusion restaurant specializing in sushi, sashimi, and hibachi. They keep it fresh, they keep it consistent, and they keep people coming back — which is why they've grown to 12 locations across Louisiana, Texas, and Florida.

## The Menu

The sushi and sashimi are the main event. Clean cuts, quality fish, and a menu broad enough to satisfy the purists and the people who want a creative roll. The hibachi side of things holds its own too. And if you haven't been for one of their all-you-can-eat specials, that's the move.

## The Vibe

Lively, welcoming, and unpretentious. It's the kind of place where you bring the whole table and everyone leaves happy — which in New Orleans, is exactly what a neighborhood spot should be.

## The Verdict

Sushi Masa isn't trying to be a downtown destination. It's a reliable, quality neighborhood Japanese restaurant that happens to do most things right. In Metairie, that's saying something.
