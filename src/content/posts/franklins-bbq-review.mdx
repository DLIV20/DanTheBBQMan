# Franklin Barbecue: Worth the 3-Hour Wait?

Short answer: yes. Long answer: also yes, but bring a chair.

## The Wait

I arrived at 8:15am for an 11am opening. There were already 40 people in line. By the time the doors opened, the line stretched around the block. This is not unusual.

## The Food

The brisket is transcendent. Fatty end, thick smoke ring, bark that crumbles at the touch. The ribs are secondary but still excellent. Get the turkey — most people sleep on it and they shouldn't.

## The Verdict

Franklin's earned every bit of its reputation. Go once. Get there early. Bring cash and someone to talk to.

**Rating: 10/10**
