# Taquerias Carnalito Is Bringing Mexico City to Poydras Street

There's a trompo spinning in the window at 930 Poydras and that alone should be enough to pull you in.

## What It Is

Taquerias Carnalito is an authentic Mexico City taquería planted right in the middle of downtown New Orleans. They're not doing Tex-Mex. They're not doing fusion. They're doing traditional trompos — vertical rotating spits of al pastor, carne asada, and sirloin — built from a family recipe that goes back to 1928.

That's not a gimmick. That's a lineage.

## The Food

The tacos are the move. Al pastor off the trompo, freshly carved and dropped onto a warm tortilla — it's the real thing. The kind you'd find in a Mexico City market, not a strip mall. The margaritas hold their own too.

## The Vibe

The design pulls from classic Mexican market taquerías — loud, warm, and unapologetic. They describe it as "not just serving tacos, we're serving a vibe," and they're not wrong. It hits different for downtown New Orleans, especially after midnight on a Friday when they're still open until 1 AM.

## The Verdict

Carnalito is exactly the kind of spot that makes New Orleans worth eating in. Authentic, bold, and built on something real.

**Hours:** Sun–Thu 11am–11pm · Fri–Sat 11am–1am · 930 Poydras St, New Orleans
