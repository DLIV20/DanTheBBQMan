# The Ultimate Smoked Brisket Guide

Low and slow is the only way. Brisket is the king of BBQ cuts, and once you nail it, nothing else compares.

## What You'll Need

- 12–14 lb whole packer brisket
- Coarse kosher salt
- Coarse black pepper
- Oak or hickory wood

## The Method

Trim fat cap to about 1/4 inch. Season generously with equal parts salt and pepper — don't be shy. Let it sit uncovered in the fridge overnight.

Smoke at 225°F until internal temp hits 165°F, then wrap in butcher paper and push through to 203°F. Rest for at least one hour before slicing.

Against the grain. Always against the grain.
