# Super Witch Ice Cream Is Casting Spells on the Crescent City

New Orleans has no shortage of places to satisfy a sweet tooth, but Super Witch Ice Cream Co. is doing something different — and people are showing up for it.

## What It Is

Super Witch is a small-batch soft serve shop with a playful, witchy aesthetic and serious dedication to flavor. Every week the menu rotates with new creations you won't find anywhere else in the city. That's the whole point — come back often, because what's on the board today won't be there next week.

## The Vibe

Pink and blue branding, a neighborhood feel, and the kind of energy that makes you want to post before you eat. They open Thursday through Sunday, 4pm to 9pm, which means they've positioned themselves perfectly as the after-dinner move.

## The Pints

Can't make it to the shop? Pints drop every Wednesday at 7pm online. Set a reminder — they go fast.

## Verdict

Super Witch isn't trying to be everything. It's doing one thing — soft serve — and doing it with intention. That's exactly the kind of spot New Orleans needs more of.

Find them on Instagram [@superwitchsoftserve](https://instagram.com/superwitchsoftserve) or hit their website for directions.
