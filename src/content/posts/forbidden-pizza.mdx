# Forbidden Pizza Is Bringing Brooklyn to Baronne Street

At 600 Baronne Street in the CBD, there's a pizza spot that's been quietly earning the title of best pizza in New Orleans — and after a visit, it's hard to argue otherwise.

## The Concept

Forbidden Pizza is New York-style thin crust done right. They import ingredients from Italy, engineer their recipes with obsessive precision, and refuse to mask mediocre product with garlic overload. The result is clean, fresh pizza where the quality of the ingredients actually speaks for itself.

## The Pies

The menu is where it gets fun. The **Original Sin** is a classic margherita done with conviction. The **Forbidden Fruit** comes with fig jam and bacon — sweet, salty, and completely disarming. The **Burrata Bliss** and **Italian Stallion** round out a white pizza section that holds its own against any red. And if you want something thicker, the **She Thicc** pan pizza is exactly what it sounds like.

![Forbidden Pizza](/images/pizza-2.jpg)

## The Vibe

Cool decor, upbeat energy, a space that feels like it belongs in a Brooklyn neighborhood but fits right into the CBD. They do Saints game day brunch on weekends when the Superdome is rocking down the street — that alone makes them essential.

## The Details

**Hours:** Thu–Mon, Noon–10pm (closed Tue–Wed)
**Location:** 600 Baronne St, New Orleans

Order online at [forbiddenpizza.com](https://www.forbiddenpizza.com) or walk in and take your chances on a busy night.

## Verdict

Best pizza in the city is a bold claim. Forbidden Pizza makes it easy to believe.
